About Us

K.K. Wagh College of Engineering Education and Research is a premier institution located in Nashik, offering quality education in various fields of engineering and technology. Established in 1984, the college is affiliated with Savitribai Phule Pune University and has gained a reputation for academic excellence, innovation, and research.

Our mission is to nurture future leaders in engineering and technology by providing students with the knowledge, skills, and practical experience necessary to excel in their careers. We strive to foster a culture of creativity, critical thinking, and problem-solving among our students.
